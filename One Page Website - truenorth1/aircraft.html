Aerial Applicators

Our fleet consist of 8 Aircraft. We are proud to announce that True North Aviation owns the biggest Air Tractor fleet in Africa! Aerial Applications is seasonal related therefore our pilots work all over the country and Africa in various areas during the different peak seasons. Aerial Application is often the only or most economic method for precision and timely pesticide, herbicides, fungicides and fertilizer application. In addition Aerial Applicators assist in forest protection applications, fire fighting and public health application services to combat life threatening pest like mosquitoes.

Helicopter Services

With our fleet of 7 aircraft our experienced team is ready to assist you!
Game Services:
Our pilots are game rated and has a great deal of experience with wildlife. When involved in game services it is important to keep stress as little as possible for the animal, and that is an integral part of our game capture operations. Our Game service includes but is not limited to: anti-poaching services, tracking of sick or wounded animals, vaccination of animals, game counts, herding and darting services. Our pilots work all over the country and neighbouring countries, but focus on the North-West, Gauteng, Limpopo and Mpumalanga. For more enquiries for a chopper in your area do not hesitate to contact us.

Flight Training Centre

True North Aviation has 4 aircraft ready to offer premium PPL to CPL training on aeroplanes and helicopters. Residential students are accommodated on the flight school campus. Aeroplane training is done on a Piper Archer and helicopter training is done on a Robinson R22 or R44. While doing helicopter training you have the opportunity to learn about and experience game conservation. Our experienced instructors are ready to assist you in any queries and to offer you an introductory flight.
